What It Is To Have Us In Your Community

At Embassy of Children's Safety, we are dedicated to stepping up our efforts in addressing maltreatment and abuse of children. Through this outreach, we visit societies that sadly rank high in the country in terms of child maltreatment. Doing that is by no means an easy feat, but through cooperation with chiefs and elders of communities, we are able to organise meetings that host several people and teach them maltreatment prevention programs and ways to discipline without abusing their wards. We are also able to visit people in the communities who have once suffered abuse and get them back on their feet through counselling, hospital check-up, mental health screening and many others. People are also taught abuse reporting and helped with the necessary contacts to ensure people who abuse or maltreat children are brought to book.
