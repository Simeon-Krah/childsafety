Education Belongs To All

As part of our activities in ensuring that the world is better for all, we make it possible for children who have once left school get back to school and continue their education. Homeless children and those hawking on the street as a result of maltreatment they suffer in their homes or due to lack of parents also get the opportunity to enroll again at schools through the provisions of our generous partners. In the near future, the Back to School Project aims at including several refugees children and many others from other countries and currently seeking asylum in our country, Ghana.
