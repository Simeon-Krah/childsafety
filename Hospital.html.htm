Greetings To The Mothers

With our organization's mission to create a safer world for all, we extend our programs to several mothers in the maternity ward at many hospitals. Because mothers are often the first group children expect protection from, we empower them with the necessary teachings needed to ensure the safety of their children at all the time. Mothers are taught, injected and energized with maltreatment prevention programs, so they are able to practise and make it possible for others to follow to provide a home and a community free from abuse and maltreatment. On some occasions too, we add to their joy by donating items to cater for the needs of their new babies.
